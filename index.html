Your Favorite Characters

Doraemon

A blue robotic cat from the 22nd century who travels back in time to help Nobita. He has a magical pocket filled with futuristic gadgets and loves dorayaki pastries. Despite being a robot, he's afraid of mice and can't resist helping his friend.He is considered to be the greatest icon in japan

Nobita Nobi

A lazy and clumsy elementary school student who struggles with academics and sports. Despite his flaws, he has a kind heart and strong sense of justice. With Doraemon's help, he learns valuable life lessons and grows as a person through various adventures

Shizuka Minamoto

A sweet and intelligent girl who is Nobita's best friend and future wife. She excels in academics and is talented in music. Known for her kindness and patience, she often helps Nobita with his problems and enjoys taking baths and playing violin.

Takeshi "Gian" Goda

The neighborhood bully who often picks on Nobita and other smaller kids. Despite his rough exterior, he cares deeply for his friends and family. He loves singing (though he's terrible at it) and has dreams of becoming a professional singer.

Suneo Honekawa

A wealthy and cunning boy who is Gian's sidekick. He often shows off his possessions and excludes Nobita from activities. Despite his arrogant nature, he occasionally shows genuine friendship and has a talent for arts and crafts.

Dorami

Doraemon's younger sister who is more advanced and reliable than her brother. She's yellow, has ears, and is excellent at academics. She occasionally visits from the future to help when Doraemon malfunctions or needs assistance with difficult situations.
